Detection Layers — on 1-min Candles
1 · Momentum Burst
≥0.25% price move in 3 candles. On 1-min candles this is a real meaningful move. Min 2 layers required.
2 · Volatility Expansion (ATR)
Candle range ≥3.5× ATR(14) on 1-min candles. Real volatility spike. Records raw tick interval at spike.
3 · Wick Rejection
Wick ≥70% of 1-min candle range. Requires ATR confirm. Real spike-and-reject on meaningful candle data.
4 · Statistical Outlier (Z-Score)
≥3.0σ from SMA20 on 1-min closes. Statistically significant move on real price data.
5 · RSI Divergence
New extreme + RSI diverging on 1-min candles. Real momentum exhaustion signal.
Prediction Layers — on 1-min Candles + Raw Ticks
P1 · ATR Compression (25%)
Current 1-min ATR vs prior 20 candles avg. Low ATR on real 1-min candles = genuine coiling. Self-reference excluded.
P2 · Overdue Pressure (20%)
RAW TICK count since last spike vs session-calibrated tick average. Independent of candles — pure timing signal.
P3 · RSI Position (15%)
RSI on 1-min closes. Mid-range 45–55 = market coiling quietly before explosive move. Real signal on real candles.
P4 · Spike Regularity (15%)
Variance of RAW TICK intervals between spikes. Low variance = predictable cycle = high confidence.
P5 · BB Squeeze (10%)
BB width on 1-min candles vs prior 20 avg. Real price compression visible on meaningful candle data.
P6 · MACD Compression (15%)
MACD histogram on 1-min candles. Last 5 bars vs prior 5 — real momentum exhaustion before spike.
Price Action — on 1-min Candles (min 3 ticks/candle)
PA1 · Inside Bar
1-min candle H/L inside previous. Only fires on candles with ≥3 real ticks — not 1-tick artifacts.
PA2 · Doji Count
3+ consecutive 1-min candles with body <25% of range, each with ≥3 ticks. Real market pause.
PA3 · HH/LL Structure
4+ consecutive 1-min candles with higher lows (BOOM) or lower highs (CRASH). Real trend loading.
PA4 · S/R Proximity
Price within 1% of recent 1-min swing high (BOOM) or swing low (CRASH). Real level test.
PA5 · MACD Histogram Shrink
3 consecutive 1-min bars with decreasing magnitude. Real momentum dying before spike.
Best setup on live feed: P1 (ATR compressed on 1-min) + P5 (BB squeezed on 1-min) + P2 (raw ticks overdue) + PA1 (inside bar) = highest confidence pre-spike signal. All layers now use real 1-min OHLC from Deriv API — not simulated or sparse tick-built candles.
